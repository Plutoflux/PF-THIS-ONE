Production Services

For production services, songwriting, recording, mixing, creative development, and release strategy inquiries, contact Pluto Flux Records directly. Richard Wicander works front to back with artists to develop records with identity, impact, and long-term vision.

Richard Wicander is a producer, publishing A&R, audio engineer, drummer, guitarist, and vocalist working across rock, metal, hip hop, and pop. Through Pluto Flux and his wider work in music, he helps artists shape records from inception to release, combining creative direction with real-world production and strategy.
